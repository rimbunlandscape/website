ℹ️ About Rimbun Landscape

Indonesia's leading sustainable landscaping company, transforming outdoor spaces with native plants since 2020

Our Story

Founded by a team of passionate landscape architects and conservation biologists, Rimbun Landscape was born from a simple belief: that beautiful outdoor spaces should enhance, not harm, the natural environment. We specialize in creating stunning landscapes using Indonesia's incredible native plant diversity.

🎯 Our Mission

To transform Indonesia's landscapes through sustainable design practices that celebrate native biodiversity while creating beautiful, functional outdoor spaces. We believe that landscaping should work in harmony with nature, not against it.

🔮 Our Vision

To be Indonesia's most trusted partner for sustainable landscaping, leading the way in native plant conservation and eco-friendly design practices.
